Day 1 – Fri Oct 10: Arrival & Paris by Night Gentle Day
  • Private transfer CDG → hotel; light lunch nearby.
  • Stroll: Tuileries or Luxembourg Gardens (optional: Musée de l’Orangerie).
  • Seine River Dinner Cruise.
  • Hotel perk: Walk to Trocadéro after the cruise for Eiffel views (steps from the hotel).
Day 2 – Sat Oct 11: Louvre, Wine & Anniversary Dinner Culture + Wine
  • Private Louvre tour (skip‑the‑line, 2–3 hrs).
  • Wine cellar tasting at Ô Chateau or Les Caves du Louvre (with cheese).
  • Leisure walk in Saint‑Germain or refresh at hotel.
  • Anniversary Dinner (Le Jules Verne or Michelin spot). Add‑on: Eiffel sparkle from Trocadéro.
Day 3 – Sun Oct 12: Versailles Day Trip & Evening Experience Day Trip
  • Guided Versailles (palace + gardens), lunch/picnic on‑site.
  • Return late afternoon, easy reset at hotel.
  • Evening: choose Crazy Horse • Sainte‑Chapelle • Jazz Club.
Day 4 – Mon Oct 13: Cooking, Montmartre & Flexible Evening Experiences
  • Morning cooking/pastry class.
  • Explore Montmartre (Sacré‑Cœur, Place du Tertre).
  • Optional photo session at Montmartre or Trocadéro.
  • Evening: stylish bistro OR your chosen show if not done Day 3.
Day 5 – Tue Oct 14: Leisure & Departure Easy Morning
  • Breakfast near hotel, last‑minute shopping on Rue de Passy.
  • Private transfer to CDG ~11:30 AM for 2:40 PM flight.
