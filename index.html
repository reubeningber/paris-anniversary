Paris Anniversary Itinerary

Home base: Hotel Maison Hamelin (16th, near Trocadéro). Each day expands to show details, a hotel‑centered map, and a Google Maps route.

✈️ Day 0 – Thu Oct 9: Flight to ParisTravel
  • 8:20 PM – Delta DL0266, depart JFK → CDG (overnight).
🇫🇷 Day 1 – Fri Oct 10: Arrival, Dinner & Sunset Seine CruiseGentle Day
  • 10:00 AM – Land at CDG → private transfer to hotel (arrive ~12:00 PM), check‑in / freshen up.
  • 12:30 PM – Light lunch near hotel:
    • Café Carette (Trocadéro) — pastries, quiche, salads.
    • Le Wilson (classic brasserie, near Trocadéro).
    • Quick boulangerie picnic (baguette sandwiches, quiche, pastries).
  • Afternoon – Optional garden stroll / rest:
    • Tuileries Garden & Musée de l’Orangerie (Monet’s Water Lilies).
    • Luxembourg Gardens — relaxed, romantic promenades & fountains.
  • 6:00 PM – Dinner near the Eiffel Tower:
    • Monsieur Bleu (Palais de Tokyo, chic views)
    • Girafe (Trocadéro) — seafood, terrace views
    • Les Ombres — rooftop glass canopy, Eiffel right above
  • 6:30–7:30 PM – Sunset Seine sightseeing cruise (not dinner):
    • Bateaux Parisiens (Eiffel Tower dock ~€18)
    • Vedettes de Paris (smaller boats, intimate)
    • Bateaux Mouches (~1h15 iconic loop)
    October sunset ≈ 6:50 PM — perfect golden hour into twilight.
  • 8:00 PM – Eiffel Tower sparkle from Trocadéro (5‑minute show at the start of each hour).
  • 9:00 PM – Return to hotel & relax.

  • Versailles guided tour (palace + gardens) – self‑travel there & back.

    • Alternative provider: Viator – Skip‑the‑Line Classic

    • Dinner at Le Bazar (kosher; rec by Sara & Ben) → restaurantlebazar.com
    • 8:30 PM – Arrive Trocadéro for Eiffel sparkle (first show around this time)
    • Quick Seine stroll or back to hotel; sparkles at the top of each hour if you’re up for it

🥐🎭 Day 4 – Mon Oct 13: Pastry Class, Notre‑Dame & Montmartre → Dinner + Crazy HorseExperiencesShowtime: 8:00 PM
  • ~9:30–12:00 PM – Pastry class (Saint‑Germain‑des‑Prés): Learn to Bake Classic French Croissants (Maison Fleuret).
  • 12:00–12:20 PM – Travel to Notre‑Dame (Île de la Cité).
  • 12:20–1:20 PM – Explore interior & exterior of Notre‑Dame. Tours/tickets: notre‑dame‑tickets.com.
  • 1:20–1:40 PM – Optional stroll to Île Saint‑Louis (café or Berthillon ice cream).
  • 1:40–2:30 PM – Head to Montmartre → Sacré‑Cœur & Place du Tertre.
  • 2:30–5:30 PM – Wander Montmartre’s lanes & cafés. Self‑guided routes:
    • On the Luce – Montmartre walking tour
    • Beauty & the Bucket List – self‑guided
  • 5:30–6:30 PM – Return to hotel to refresh.
  • 6:30–8:00 PM – Elegant dinner (central Paris). Option: Girafe (Trocadéro).
  • 8:00 PM show (~9:30 PM end) – Crazy Horse cabaret show → book tickets.
  • After – Optional Seine stroll, nightcap, or one last Eiffel sparkle.
